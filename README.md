# Builders Stream Pro (BSP)

**Cloud-based project management software built specifically for small renovation and construction contractors.**

## Overview

Builders Stream Pro is a SaaS platform designed to solve the operational chaos facing the 450,000+ small construction and renovation companies in the US. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, text messages, and multiple disconnected tools, contractors get one integrated platform for estimating, scheduling, job costing, change orders, client communication, and financial management.

**Launch Target:** Q1 2027  
**Founder:** Douglas Davis | Builders Stream  
**Stage:** Pre-Launch Planning & Market Validation

---

## The Problem

Small contractors and renovation firms (1–50 employees, $500K–$5M annual revenue) face daily operational pain:

- **Estimation is manual & error-prone** — 4–8 hours per estimate in Excel; 2–4% revenue loss to estimation errors
- **Change orders create disputes** — Scope creep documented via text; contractors often eat unexpected costs
- **Client communication is exhausting** — Homeowners call constantly wanting status updates
- **Financial data is fragmented** — QuickBooks integration breaks; real-time job profitability is invisible
- **Current tools don't fit** — Enterprise solutions ($500–800/month) are overkill; cheap alternatives don't understand renovation workflows

---

## The Solution

Builders Stream Pro provides:

- **Renovation-Specific Estimating** — Templates and workflows built for how remodeling actually works
- **Integrated Job Costing** — Real-time visibility into project profitability vs. budget
- **Change Order Management** — Formal documentation, approval workflows, and automatic invoice updates
- **Client Portal** — Reduce communication overhead; homeowners track progress independently
- **QuickBooks Sync** — Reliable, bi-directional integration; no duplicate transactions or silent failures
- **Mobile-First Field Operations** — Time tracking, daily logs, photo documentation from the job site
- **CRM & Lead Management** — Built for construction sales cycles, not generic CRM

## Competitive Landscape

| Tool | Price | Best For | Why BSP is Different |
|------|-------|----------|----------------------|
| **Procore** | $500–$800+/month | Enterprise construction | Too expensive, designed for new construction at scale |
| **Buildertrend** | $499–$799/month | Residential builders | Overkill for small crews; annual price increases |
| **JobTread** | $159–$249/month | General contracting | Generic workflows; not renovation-specific |
| **Contractor Foreman** | $49–$415/month | SMB contractors | Affordable but unreliable; poor UX & mobile |
| **Spreadsheets + QB** | Free | DIY contractors | No automation; breaks at >3 people or >5 projects |

**BSP's Edge:**
- Built by someone with 25 years of software engineering + 10+ years in construction
- Designed specifically for renovation workflows (not generic project management)
- Pricing that works for working owners (~$79/month vs. $500+/month)
- Reliable, purpose-built QuickBooks integration
- Customer acquisition via self-serve (no enterprise sales required)
